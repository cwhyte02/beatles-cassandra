Ringo Starr

Ringo Starr is an English musician, songwriter and actor who achieved international fame as the drummer for the Beatles.

  • Beatles

Ringo Starr occasionally sang lead vocals with the group, usually for one song on each album, including "Yellow Submarine" and "With a Little Help from My Friends".
